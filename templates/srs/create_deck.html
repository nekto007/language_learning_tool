How to Organize Decks

Decks help you organize your vocabulary by topics or difficulty level. Here are some tips:

Ideas for organizing decks:

  • By topic - create decks for different thematic groups (travel, business, food, technology)
  • By source - separate decks for vocabulary from different books or courses
  • By difficulty level - basic vocabulary, advanced vocabulary, etc.
  • By word type - complex phrases, idioms, phrasal verbs
Note: By default, all words with "Active" status appear in the "Main Deck". You can move words between decks as you learn.
